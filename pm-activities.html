Competitive eSports

The competitive eSports team meets outside of Flex Day. Practices and matches are held in the afternoon with no required attendance during flex day. If you are interested in joining the Competitive eSports team, please see Mr. Jernigan.


Afternoon Activities
SCA SCA is a student leadership group that organizes activities both in and out of school. They help coordinate events such as homecoming, prom, and spirit weeks.
National Honor Society National Honor Society recognizes the academic success of students at CodeRVA.
CS Honor Society Computer Science Honor Society recognizes the academic success of students at CodeRVA. A community service component also is required of members.
eSports(Casual) eSports provides students an opportunity to get together and play games. These can include games such as Super Smash Bros, Mario Kart, Street Fighter, or others.
Music Club Many students have music as a hobby, and this group offers them a chance to play, share, and jam with their friends.
Capture the Flag Capture the flag students participate in various challenges designed to develop cybersecurity skills.
Board Game Club Board games are a great way to spend time with friends, laugh, and compete in a friendly way.
