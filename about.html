What is Flex Day?


Flex Day is an opportunity to build community at CodeRVA. No classes are held on Flex Days. An extended PLT allows for work on virtual courses and meetings with counselors. Afternoon activities include clubs and other fun things.


Important Reminders

  • During morning activities, please sit in your Rotation 3 location
  • Please have a book already checked out before SSR time at 10:40am
  • If an event appears on your calendar, attendance is not optional. Your attendance will be taken.
  • You must attend the afternoon flex activities appearing on your calendar.
